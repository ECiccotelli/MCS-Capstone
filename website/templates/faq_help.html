FAQs / Help

How do I access the text to speech feature?

Click the checkbox in the top right of the page then highlight text with your cursor you wish to have read out loud.
To turn it off simply uncheck the box and your page will refresh.

How do I hide and remove a course from my schedule?

To hide a course from your schedule uncheck the box in the "My Course List" section. To remove the course entirely, scroll down and uncheck it from the "Courses" table.

Can I come back later to finish making my schedule?

Yes, you must sign in using your Manhattan College Email to have the ability to save your schedule. Once signed in click the "Save Schedule" button in the top right. Once you do that, anytime you come back the saved courses will be displayed on your page. Remember to Save!

Do I have to log in to make my schedule?

No, you can still make your schedule however it will not save and will erase once you close your browser.

I'm having trouble finding my class. Why can I not find it?

Remember to use the search function in the courses list to easily find classes you are looking for. If you still can not find your course try again later as our courses update hourly.

How do I turn this into a registration form?

Go to the Registration tab on the left side of the page. A registration form will auto generate with your courses. If you are signed in you will have the ability to also auto fill information, create an email draft, and send an email to your respective advisor for approval.

Information Help / Description

On the Dashboard tab of the website is where the schedule creation and design happens. Here the user will search for classes from the courses list at the bottom of the page and then add them to their schedule by ticking the checkbox labeled "add/remove" to the right of the course. Once a course is added to your schedule it will be listed under "My Course List" and displayed on the schedule graphic. You can add a maximum of eight classes before a pop up appears telling you the max course limit has been reached. If you try to add classes that have a time conflict another pop up will appear alerting you to which two classes the conflict is with.

Once you are ready to begin the registration process you can head over to the registration tab, however, if you wish to save your schedule in order to view or edit it at a later time you can sign in using your Manhattan College Email and press the "Save Schedule" button. If you have an existing schedule tied to your account, the existing schedule will have priority when saving. 

In the Registration tab as long as you have selected your classes previously in the Dashboard tab, they will auto fill into the PDF registration document. If you are signed in you will have the ability to complete auto fill other personal data. You will also have the ability where the website can auto sign the document with your permission. You will also have the option to email it to your advisor or save the email as a draft in your Manhattan College Gmail Account.
